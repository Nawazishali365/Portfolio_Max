I work across three areas: cloud strategy and Azure architecture (solution design, migration, governance), GenAI and Microsoft Copilot (readiness, deployment, adoption), and Microsoft 365 Modern Work (Teams, Power Platform, change management). Engagements range from focused advisory to full programme leadership.

Every engagement starts with a discovery phase to understand your technology landscape, business priorities, and adoption gaps. From there I design a tailored roadmap and support execution and change management — ensuring your teams actually adopt the technology and realize value.

Microsoft's platform is deeply integrated, enterprise-grade, and AI-native. Azure, M365, Copilot, Teams, and Power Platform form a coherent stack that most large organizations already have. The challenge isn't choosing the platform — it's unlocking the value already sitting in your estate. That's exactly where I operate.

My background is enterprise — PostNord, Saab, and Microsoft's largest Nordic clients. I'm most effective in organizations with 500+ employees where cloud adoption, AI deployment, and change management have real complexity and real stakes.

Adoption starts before deployment. I assess organizational readiness, design a governance framework, identify high-value use cases, and build an enablement programme that moves users from awareness to daily habit. Copilot licenses without a change management strategy deliver almost nothing — that's the gap I close.

I've spent 13+ years inside Microsoft, which means I understand the platform from the inside — not just from a customer or partner perspective. Combined with an MBA, a Masters from KTH, and hands-on delivery at companies like CGI, PostNord, and Saab, I bring both strategic depth and practical execution capability.
